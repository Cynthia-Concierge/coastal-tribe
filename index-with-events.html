About Us

Coastal Tribe is a vibrant Jewish community bringing together people who value authentic connection, meaningful tradition, and shared celebration.

Founded by family, we create spaces where strangers become friends and friends become family. Through our Shabbat dinners and community events, we blend timeless Jewish traditions with modern, inclusive gatherings that nourish both body and soul.

Whether you're deeply connected to your Jewish roots or just beginning to explore, you're welcome at our table. Join us in building a community that celebrates heritage, fosters belonging, and creates moments that matter.
